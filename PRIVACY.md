## 1. What this app does

E² Teaching Framework ("the App") is a teaching-companion tool that helps
educators plan lessons, design term blueprints, build rubrics, and assess
student engagement. The App runs entirely in your browser (or as an installed
PWA / native app) and stores your work on your device.

## 2. Data we collect

**We do not operate any servers that collect your personal information, lesson
content, or student data.** Specifically:

| Data                           | Collected?   | Where it lives           |
|--------------------------------|--------------|--------------------------|
| Your name / email              | ❌ No        | —                        |
| Lesson plans, blueprints, notes| ❌ No (we don't see them) | Your device's local storage |
| Student names or grades        | ❌ No (we don't see them) | Your device's local storage |
| Usage analytics                | ❌ No         | —                        |
| Crash reports                  | ❌ No         | —                        |
| Device identifiers             | ❌ No         | —                        |

The only exceptions, which are **opt-in by your action**:

- **License verification.** If you purchase a paid license, your license key
  and (if applicable) the email associated with your purchase are sent to
  our payment processor (Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy) to verify validity. Read
  their privacy policies:
  - Gumroad: https://gumroad.com/privacy
  - Lemon Squeezy: https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy
- **Google Fonts.** Font files load from `fonts.googleapis.com` so the
  interface renders correctly. Google may log the IP of anyone loading
  these fonts. See https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 3. Data you enter in the App

Lesson plans, term blueprints, checklist ticks, and any other content you
type into the App are stored **locally on your device** using browser
`localStorage`. This data:

- **Never leaves your device** unless you explicitly export it (CSV, PDF,
  clipboard) and share it yourself.
- **Is not encrypted by us.** Anyone with access to your unlocked device can
  read it. Use your device's lock screen and OS-level encryption.
- **Is deleted if you clear your browser data** or uninstall the app.
- **Is not backed up by us.** Export regularly if the data matters.

## 4. Student data (FERPA / education considerations)

If you enter real student names, grades, or other identifying information
into the App, those entries are **on your device only** and we do not have
access to them. **You are the controller** of any student data you enter.

You are responsible for complying with FERPA (US), GDPR (EU), PIPEDA
(Canada), and your school/district's data-handling policies. The App does
not transmit student data to us or to any third party.

## 5. Children's privacy

The App is designed for educators. We do not knowingly allow anyone under 13
to create a license or use the paid features. The App does not collect data
from children directly — only what a teacher enters about their own classes.

## 6. Cookies / tracking

The App uses **no cookies** and **no third-party tracking**. The service
worker caches static files (HTML, CSS, images) on your device to make the
App work offline — this is purely a local cache and is not sent anywhere.

## 7. Security

We use HTTPS for all downloads and API calls (license verification, fonts).
Locally-stored data is only as secure as your device. Lock your device,
keep your OS updated, and don't share your account on shared computers.

## 8. Your rights

Because we don't collect your data, there is nothing for us to delete on
your behalf. To remove all data the App has stored on your device:

1. Open the App
2. Open your browser's settings → "Site settings" or "Clear data"
3. Clear storage for this site

For the license-server data held by Gumroad/Lemon Squeezy, you can request
deletion directly from them using their privacy tools.

**EU users (GDPR):** Your lawful basis for the limited data we do touch
(license key + processor-held purchase email) is **contract performance** —
we need it to validate that you're entitled to use the paid app.

## 9. International users

The App is sold globally. If you purchase a license, your payment is processed
by Gumroad (USA) or Lemon Squeezy (Ireland), who handle cross-border
transfers. Their privacy policies govern that transfer.
