Pillar 1 — Lesson Design (5-Part Blueprint)

  • Hook (2 min): Mystery question, surprising fact, video, real-world problem
  • Teach (10-15 min): Chunked content, visuals, think-aloud modeling
  • Practice (15-20 min): Guided then independent, partner work, hands-on
  • Connect (5 min): Real-world relevance, cross-curricular links
  • Reflect (5 min): Exit tickets, journals, 3-2-1 summary
  • Scaffolding: Break into steps, sentence starters, worked examples, I Do/We Do/You Do

Pillar 2 — Differentiated Instruction

  • Auditory: Discussions, read-alouds, songs, podcasts
  • Visual: Diagrams, color-coding, videos, mind maps
  • Kinesthetic: Hands-on, movement breaks, manipulatives, role-play
  • Tiered Tasks: Struggling / on-level / advanced versions
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Diverse examples, honor home languages
  • Student Choice: Menu boards, choice in how to demonstrate learning

Pillar 3 — Engagement & Motivation (6 Levers)

  • 1. Curiosity: Mysteries, "What if...?" questions
  • 2. Active Participation: Think-pair-share, mini whiteboards, polls
  • 3. Purpose: Explain the "why", connect to student goals
  • 4. Feedback: Immediate, specific, growth-oriented
  • 5. Gamification: Points, badges, challenges, progress tracking
  • 6. Routine & Novelty: Predictable structure + surprising elements

Pillar 4 — Classroom Culture

  • Relationships: Learn names fast, 2x10 strategy, greet at door
  • Co-Created Norms: Involve students, revisit regularly, display visually
  • Restorative Discipline: Repair over punishment, reflection circles
  • Physical Environment: Flexible seating, organized materials, welcoming
