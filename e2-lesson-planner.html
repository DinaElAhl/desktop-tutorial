2 The 5-Part Blueprint — Lesson Design

🎯 HOOK

2-3 minutes
Show examples
  • Mystery question or surprising fact
  • Short video clip or image
  • "What would happen if...?" scenario
  • Quick poll or provocative statement
  • Real-world problem to solve

📚 TEACH

10-15 minutes
Show examples
  • Direct instruction with visuals
  • Think-aloud modeling
  • Interactive lecture with questions
  • Demonstration or experiment
  • Chunked content with checks

✏️ PRACTICE

15-20 minutes
Show examples
  • Guided practice → independent work
  • Partner or group activities
  • Hands-on tasks or labs
  • Problem sets with scaffolding
  • Creative projects

🔗 CONNECT

5 minutes
Show examples
  • Career connections
  • Cross-curricular links
  • Student personal experiences
  • Current events

💭 REFLECT

5 minutes
Show examples
  • Exit ticket
  • Journal entry
  • 3-2-1 summary
  • "What stuck?" discussion
  • Quick quiz
